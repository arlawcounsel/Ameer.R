About

Ameer Riaz is an enrolled Advocate focusing on Technology Law, Data Protection, and Digital Compliance advisory.

Ameer Riaz is a 22-year-old enrolled Advocate and founder of AR Law Counsel, focusing on Cyber Law, Data Privacy, and Civil Litigation. As a young legal professional, he represents a new generation of lawyers working at the intersection of law, technology, and regulatory compliance.

Through AR Law Counsel, he assists organisations, startups, and individuals in navigating complex digital regulatory frameworks, cyber dispute resolution, and civil legal proceedings while ensuring alignment with evolving Indian and international legal standards.

His practice integrates governance, risk management, technology law advisory, and litigation strategy, with a focus on emerging legal challenges in the digital ecosystem.
